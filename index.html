Who We Are

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute dedicated to building world-class professionals in digital, cyber and AI risk management.

IDR was founded by leading risk practitioners from financial services and critical infrastructure sectors. We bridge the gap between academic theory and mission-critical practice.

Through deep partnerships with UK universities and major industry players, we deliver intensive bootcamps, applied research, and direct talent deployment into regulated environments where failure is not an option.

Our Promise:
Every graduate leaves with both a certificate and a portfolio of real client projects. 92% of our alumni are placed within 90 days.

Our Three Pillars

We operate across training, innovation and advisory — creating a complete ecosystem for digital risk excellence.
